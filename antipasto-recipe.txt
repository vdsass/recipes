Antipasto

Ingredients:
1/2 pound fresh mushrooms
1 to 2 six-ounce jars marinated artichokes
1 15-ounce can pitted black olives
1 jar large stuffed green olives
1/2 red onion, sliced
3 Tbsp parmesan cheese, grated
3 Tbsp parsley, chopped

Marinade:
1 cup olive oil
1 1/2 cups wine vinegar
2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp ground pepper
1 clove garlic, minced

Combine marinade ingredients and boil for one minute.

Using marinade recipe, marinate mushrooms overnight. Add artichokes and olives and toss. Top with onion, cheese and parseley.
